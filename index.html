Kidstime Workshops

Kidstime Foundation

After coming to the realisation of the lack of support and awareness in regards to to the difficulties of being a child of someone suffering from a mental health issue, my creative partner and I decided to design an activity-based workshop collection of carefully constructed activities that we had created through research with the intention for each activity to teach children important lessons about emotional well-being and attempt to end the stigma against mental health by starting a discussion at an early age.

The main focus of this project was the creation of Our Favourite Place, a children’s book that followed the story of a young girl named Alice understanding and accepting her mother’s problems with mental health issues. I was the art director and illustrator throughout this project and produced a series of illustrations to tell the story written by copywriter Tom Baptie.
